We update our database regularly to ensure accuracy. Performance data is updated annually after official releases, inspection reports are added within 48 hours of publication, and school characteristics are refreshed monthly.

Data Processing & Quality

All data undergoes rigorous processing and validation before being displayed on MeilleureEcole.fr:

  • Validation: Cross-reference multiple sources to ensure accuracy
  • Standardization: Convert all data to consistent formats and scales
  • Gap Analysis: Identify and clearly mark incomplete data
  • Historical Tracking: Maintain historical records for trend analysis
  • Quality Checks: Automated and manual reviews for data integrity

Data Limitations

While we strive for completeness and accuracy, please be aware of these limitations:

  • Some schools may have incomplete data due to their size, type, or recent establishment
  • Private schools may have limited publicly available data
  • Data is updated periodically and may not reflect very recent changes
  • Inspection ratings may be several years old for some schools
  • Different UK nations use different assessment systems and metrics

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is your school data?

Our data comes directly from government sources including the Department for Education, Ofsted, and local authorities. We update our database regularly to ensure accuracy.

Can I update incorrect information about a school?

If you notice incorrect information, please use the contact form above and select "Report an Issue". Include the school name, URN, and specific details about what needs correction.

How are school ratings calculated?

Our ratings combine multiple factors including academic performance, Ofsted ratings, attendance, and student progress. Learn more on our About page.

Is FindSchool.uk free to use?

Yes! FindSchool.uk is completely free for all users. We believe every family should have access to comprehensive school information.

How can schools claim their profile?

School administrators can contact us using the form above and select "Partnership Opportunity" to discuss profile management options.

Do you cover private schools?

Yes, we include both state and independent (private) schools that are registered with the Department for Education across England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.

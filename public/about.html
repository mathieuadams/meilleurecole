About MeilleureEcole.fr

Empowering families with comprehensive school information to make informed educational choices across the United Kingdom

Our Mission

MeilleureEcole.fr is dedicated to providing families with transparent, comprehensive, and accessible information about schools throughout the United Kingdom. Formerly known as UKSchoolRatings.co.uk, our refreshed name reflects a wider mission to help every parent quickly find the best-fit school. We believe that every child deserves an excellent education, and every parent deserves the tools to find the right school for their child.

Our platform brings together official government data, inspection reports, performance metrics, and community reviews to create the most complete picture of UK schools available online. We're committed to making school research simple, informative, and empowering for all families.

🎯 Comprehensive Data

📊 Fair Rankings

What We Do

School Profiles

Every school profile includes essential information such as contact details, leadership, student demographics, and location. We present academic performance data including English, Maths, and Science scores, alongside attendance rates and student progress measures. Our profiles feature the latest Ofsted or equivalent inspection ratings with detailed breakdowns.

Fair Rating System

Our 1-10 rating scale combines multiple data points to create fair comparisons. We consider academic achievement, inspection results, attendance rates, and student progress. Schools with incomplete data are clearly marked, and we show which factors contribute to each rating to maintain full transparency.

Local Insights

Browse schools by city or local authority to understand educational options in your area. Compare schools side-by-side to make informed decisions. Our platform shows nearby alternatives and provides demographic context to help you understand each school's unique community.

Data Sources

MeilleureEcole.fr aggregates data from authoritative government sources to ensure accuracy and reliability. Our primary data comes from:

  • Department for Education (DfE) performance tables
  • Ofsted inspection reports and ratings
  • School census data
  • Local authority education departments
  • Education Scotland (for Scottish schools)
  • Estyn (for Welsh schools)
  • Education and Training Inspectorate (for Northern Ireland schools)

All data is processed and presented in compliance with UK data protection regulations and educational data guidelines.
